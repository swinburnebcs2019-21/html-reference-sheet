Headings and Paragraphs

Headings and paragraphs are BLOCK elements

Headings

There are 6 levels of heading. Do not skip levels. If you want to change the size of a heading, don't change its level - change the CSS.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs contain the body text of a web page. You cannot put another block element inside a paragraph.

Special Characters

HTML supports special characters. For example ® and ©.

Lists

There are two types of lists: unordered and ordered
